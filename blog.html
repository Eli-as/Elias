Sheger, Ethiopia's beauty.

Emperror Minilk and Emperres Taiytu riding a horse, portrait.

For a country that has thousands of years of history, it may sound a little bit confusing to hear that its capital city is only 132 years old. The thing of the matter is Ethiopia had many capital cities over the millenniums, Gonder(My home town) being one of the prominent ones. nevertheless, Sheger(Addis Ababa), The 132 years old city is our capital. pioneered by the Emperror Minilk and Emperres Taiytu in the year 1888
